Worked as a U.S. Attorney

Joe Lewis is running for a Senate seat to represent the state of Arizona. He is building a campaign focused on priorities like immigration reform, healthcare, infrastructure investment, entitlement programs, and gender equality. His goal is to appeal to key voter demographics in Arizona such as the Latinx population, retirees/seniors, and the urban working class. If elected, he plans to support policies around pathways to citizenship, affordable healthcare access, infrastructure stimulus spending, securing entitlement programs, and equal pay.
